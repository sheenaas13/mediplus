Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Our Frequently Asked Questions section is designed to help you find quick and clear information about our services, treatments, and processes. Whether you're curious about appointments, consultations, or specialized care, you'll find guidance to make your experience smooth and stress-free.

Do I need an appointment?
+
Most basic screenings are available on a walk-in basis. For specialized tests (like comprehensive blood panels) we recommend booking an appointment to ensure availability and privacy.
How long will results take?
+
Point-of-care tests such as blood glucose or rapid flu tests often provide results within minutes. Tests sent to a lab (e.g., some PCR tests) may take 24–72 hours depending on the service selected.
Is the testing confidential?
+
Yes. We follow privacy standards and only share results with the patient unless explicit consent is given to release information to others or to your physician.
How long does the consultation process take?
+
Most consultations take between 15 to 30 minutes depending on the complexity of your concern. Our team ensures you get detailed attention without unnecessary delays.
Can I book an appointment online?
+
Yes, you can easily schedule appointments online through our platform. Simply choose your preferred date and time, and you’ll receive an instant confirmation.
Are your services available 24/7?
+
We provide round-the-clock support for urgent queries and have flexible slots for regular consultations. This ensures you can reach us anytime you need guidance.
