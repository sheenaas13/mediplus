Overview

At MedPlus Pharmacy, we aim to provide every customer with safe, high-quality pharmaceutical and healthcare products. Our Return & Refund Policy outlines the rights, obligations, and procedures for returning or requesting refunds on purchases made through our website or retail outlets.

We understand that certain situations may arise where a product is damaged, expired, or not what you expected. To make this process as fair and transparent as possible, our policy clearly defines how returns, refunds, and replacements are handled to maintain both customer satisfaction and regulatory compliance.

Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase. By shopping with MedPlus, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms mentioned herein.

Return Conditions

Only products that are unused, unopened, and in their original packaging can be returned. This includes maintaining the original labels, seals, and barcodes. Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery along with the original invoice.

Products that have been opened, used, or tampered with cannot be accepted due to safety and hygiene regulations, especially for prescription medicines, personal care products, and temperature-sensitive items like insulin or vaccines.

MedPlus reserves the right to reject a return request if the product appears used or damaged after delivery. Our team may request supporting photos or videos for verification before approving a return.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, MedPlus will notify you via email or SMS regarding the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed automatically to your original payment method within 7â€“10 business days.

For prepaid orders, the amount will be refunded directly to your bank account or wallet. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds will be issued via bank transfer or UPI after collecting bank details from the customer.

In the event that MedPlus initiates a refund due to an operational issue (such as product unavailability or shipment delays), you will be notified and refunded immediately without requiring a return request.

Order Cancellations

Orders can be canceled before they are dispatched. Once the order is shipped, cancellation requests will not be accepted. However, you can still initiate a return after receiving the product if it meets our eligibility criteria.

MedPlus reserves the right to cancel any order due to unavailability of stock, payment issues, prescription verification failure, or incorrect information provided during checkout. Customers will be notified of cancellations and eligible refunds will be processed promptly.

For subscription-based medicine deliveries, you may cancel the upcoming cycle anytime before the dispatch date. Refunds for prepaid subscription orders will be handled in accordance with the refund process above.

Non-returnable Items

Certain items are not eligible for return or refund due to health, safety, or regulatory restrictions. These include:

  • Opened or used medicines, even if partially consumed.
  • Temperature-sensitive products such as insulin, vaccines, and biologicals.
  • Personal care products like creams, lotions, or hygiene items once unsealed.
  • Customized compounded medicines or prescriptions filled on request.

We encourage customers to review the product description carefully and consult our pharmacists before placing an order for any specialized or non-returnable products.
